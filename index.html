How it Works

  • You pay us a flat fee of $399 and we become your listing agent
  • Since you are on the MLS you are required to offer a commission to the buyers agent (amount is up to you)
  • Agents will contact you directly. Any email or phone leads we receive will be forwarded to you
  • All offers from Agents will be received by us and emailed directly to you ASAP.
  • You can cancel at anytime

Whats included

  • Your Listing on the MLS
  • Your Listing on all the popular Real Estate websites (Realtor.com, Zillow, Redfin, etc)
  • A Realtor who will be available to you (via phone or email) from list to close
  • Professional Comparative Market Analysis
  • Professionally installed Yard Sign
  • Unlimited changes to your listing
  • Unlimited Open House Advertising
  • Sales Contracts and Disclosure Forms
